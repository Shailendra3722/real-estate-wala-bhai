# Microcopy Quick Reference Sheet

**Use this for copy-paste when building pages**

---

## 🗺️ Map

**Permission:**
- Title: "Help us find homes near you"
- Allow: "Yes, show nearby homes"
- Skip: "I'll search myself"

**Pin Drop:**
- Active: "Tap the map to drop your pin"
- Success: "Pin placed! We'll search for homes here."

**Buttons:**
- Filter: "Filter Homes"
- Pin: "Drop a Pin"
- Recenter: "Back to my location"

## 📌 Empty States

**No Saved:**
- "No saved homes yet"
- "When you find a home you like, save it here"
- Button: "Start Browsing"

**No Results:**
- "No homes found"
- "Try searching in a different area or change your filters"

---

## ✨ Tone Reminders

✅ DO:
- "Help us find homes for you"
- "We'll show you properties nearby"
- "Your details are safe with us"

❌ DON'T:
- "Initiate property verification"
- "Submit geolocation parameters"
- "Configure search filters"

---

**Remember:** Friendly • Trust-building • Simple English
