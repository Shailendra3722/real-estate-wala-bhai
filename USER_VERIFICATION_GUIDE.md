# How to Get Verified

## Why Get Verified?

Getting verified on Real Estate Wala Bhai helps build trust with buyers and sellers.

**Benefits:**
- ✅ **3x More Inquiries** - Verified users get more responses
- ✅ **Trust Badge** - Blue checkmark on all your listings
- ✅ **Stand Out** - Shows you're a serious, verified user
- ✅ **Safe & Private** - Your ID is encrypted and deleted after 30 days

## How It Works

### Simple 3-Step Process

```
1. Upload ID → 2. We Verify → 3. Get Badge
   (2 minutes)    (24 hours)     (Forever!)
```

---

## Step-by-Step Guide

### Step 1: Choose Your ID

Pick any **government-issued ID**:
- ✅ Aadhaar Card
- ✅ PAN Card
- ✅ Driving License
- ✅ Passport

**You only need ONE of these.**

---

### Step 2: Take a Clear Photo

**Photo Guidelines:**
- 📸 **Clear & Readable** - All text should be visible
- ☀️ **Good Lighting** - No shadows or dark areas
- 📏 **Not Cropped** - Show full ID card
- 🚫 **No Blur** - Use a steady hand or surface

**Good Example:**
```
✅ All corners visible
✅ Text is clear
✅ No glare
✅ Flat surface
```

**Bad Example:**
```
❌ Blurry photo
❌ Cropped edges
❌ Too dark
❌ Glare/reflection
```

---

### Step 3: Upload on App

1. Go to **My Profile** → **Get Verified**
2. Select your ID type (Aadhaar/PAN/etc.)
3. Upload the photo
4. Enter last 4 digits of ID number
5. Click **Submit for Verification**

**That's it!** ✅

---

## What Happens Next?

### You'll See: "Verification Pending"

**Timeline:**
- ⏱️ **0-24 hours:** Our team reviews your ID
- ✅ **Approved:** You get a verified badge
- 📧 **Email:** We notify you either way

**While You Wait:**
- You can still use the app normally
- Your listings are visible
- You just won't have the badge yet

**You'll Get:**
- Blue **✓ Verified** badge next to your name
- Badge shows on all your property listings

## If Rejected ❌

**Don't Worry!** You can try again.

**Common Reasons:**
- Photo was blurry/unclear
- Name didn't match your profile
- Last 4 digits were wrong
- ID was cropped or incomplete

**What to Do:**
1. Read the rejection reason in email
2. Fix the issue (take clearer photo, etc.)
3. Upload again

**No limit on attempts!** We want you to succeed.

## Privacy & Safety

### Your Data is Safe 🔒

**We Protect Your Privacy:**
- 🔐 **Encrypted Storage** - Your ID is encrypted (AES-256)
- 👀 **Team Only** - Only our verification team sees it
- 🗑️ **Auto-Delete** - Deleted after 30 days
- 🚫 **Never Shared** - Not sold or shared with anyone

**What We Store:**
- ✅ Verification status (verified/not verified)
- ✅ Last 4 digits of ID (e.g., "1234")
- ❌ NOT your full ID number
- ❌ NOT the photo (deleted after 30 days)

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Is verification mandatory?
**A:** No, but verified users get 3x more inquiries!

### Q: How long does it take?
**A:** Usually within 24 hours, often much faster.

### Q: What if my name is slightly different on ID?
**A:** Small variations are OK (e.g., "Rajesh Kumar" vs "Rajesh K"). Major differences may be rejected.

### Q: Can I use my voter ID?
**A:** Currently we accept: Aadhaar, PAN, Driving License, and Passport. More IDs coming soon!

### Q: Is my ID safe?
**A:** Yes! It's encrypted, only seen by our team, and deleted after 30 days.

### Q: Can I delete my verification?
**A:** Yes, go to Profile → Settings → Delete Verification.

### Q: What if I change my phone number?
**A:** Your verification stays valid. Update your number in profile settings.

### Q: Do I need to verify again?
**A:** No, once verified you stay verified (unless you change major account details).

## Tips for Quick Approval

**✅ Do This:**
- Use natural daylight or bright room light
- Place ID on dark background (easier to read)
- Take photo from directly above (no angle)
- Make sure last 4 digits match what you enter
- Use your real name that matches ID

**❌ Avoid This:**
- Don't blur or edit the photo
- Don't crop out any part of ID
- Don't use flash (causes glare)
- Don't submit someone else's ID
- Don't enter wrong last 4 digits
