## ✨ Microcopy Principles

### Do's ✅
- Use "you" and "your" (personal)
- Keep sentences short (under 10 words)
- Use everyday words
- Show empathy and understanding
- Build trust with transparency
- Use emojis sparingly for warmth

### Don'ts ❌
- Avoid technical jargon
- Don't use real estate terms without explaining
- Never be pushy or aggressive
- Don't make promises you can't keep
- Avoid negative language

### Examples

**Instead of:** "Initiate property verification process"  
**Say:** "Get your property verified"

**Instead of:** "Submit inquiry"  
**Say:** "Show interest"

**Instead of:** "Filter parameters"  
**Say:** "Refine your search"

**Instead of:** "Geolocation services required"  
**Say:** "We need your location to help you"

## Summary

**Keep it:**
- 👥 Friendly - Talk like a helpful friend
- 🛡️ Trust-building - Show you care about safety
- 🗣️ Simple - Use everyday words
- ❤️ Human - Show empathy and warmth

**Remember:**
Every word should help users feel confident, safe, and excited about finding their dream home!
