Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2026

We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights under Indian law — including the Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act, 2023.

1. Data We Collect

Data Type What We Collect Why
Account Info Name, email address, password (hashed) To create and manage your account
Profile Data Phone number, user role (buyer/owner) To personalize your experience
Listing Data Property details, photos, location, price To publish your property listings
Usage Data Pages visited, search queries, click patterns To improve our platform
Device Data Browser type, IP address, OS For security and fraud prevention

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide the service — showing you relevant listings and letting you publish properties
  • To improve the platform — understanding how users interact with features
  • For security — preventing fraudulent accounts and listings
  • To communicate — sending important account notifications (not spam)

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. Ever.

3. Third-Party Services We Use

  • Google Firebase — Authentication, database (Firestore), and file storage. Firebase Privacy Policy →
  • Google Fonts — Typography assets loaded from Google's CDN
  • Vercel — Website hosting and CDN delivery. Vercel Privacy Policy →

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored in Google Firebase Firestore in the asia-south1 (Mumbai) region — your data stays in India. We use industry-standard encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest.

Passwords are hashed using Firebase Authentication — we never store your password in plain text.

5. Your Rights (DPDP Act 2023) Indian Law

Under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023, you have the right to:

  • Access — Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — Update or correct your personal data (via Settings page)
  • Erasure — Delete your account and all associated data (via Settings → Security → Delete Account)
  • Grievance Redressal — Raise complaints about data handling with us directly

To exercise these rights, email us at privacy@realestatewalabhai.com.

6. Cookies

We use essential browser storage (localStorage) to maintain your login session and preferences. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

7. Children's Privacy

Our platform is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If you believe a minor has registered, please contact us immediately.

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal data is permanently removed from our systems within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it by law.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or an in-app notice. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page always reflects the most recent revision.

10. Contact & Grievance Officer

For privacy-related queries or to exercise your data rights under the DPDP Act, contact us:

  • Email: privacy@realestatewalabhai.com
  • Response time: Within 72 hours on business days
  • Platform: Real Estate Wala Bhai
